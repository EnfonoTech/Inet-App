3.1 My Dashboard /pms/im-dashboard

Personal operations overview. Auto-refreshes every 5 minutes.

KPI tiles (6)

TileDescription
Total Assigned SitesAll sites dispatched to your teams in the selected period
Completed SitesSites with Completed execution status (green)
In ProgressSites currently in execution (blue)
Delayed SitesSites past planned date with no completion (red)
Today's TargetSites planned for today (indigo)
Today CompletedSites completed today (green)

Project Progress panel (left 2/3): Progress bar per project — green ≥70%, amber ≥40%, blue below 40%.

Team Performance panel (right 1/3): Up to 8 teams with sites done count.

Site Status table (left 2/3): Site ID, Location, Project, Status badge, Last Update ("Xm ago"), Issue flag. "View all →" link goes to Rollout Execution.

Issues & Escalations panel (right 1/3): Critical Issues (red dot), Pending Approvals (amber dot), Open Dummy POs (purple dot), QC Fail Needs Action (green dot).

Site Map (1/3): Leaflet interactive map — green=Completed, blue=In Progress, red=Issue/Cancelled, amber=Hold, yellow=Planned. Click a pin for a popup.

My Performance panel (1/3): Monthly Target (sites), Achieved (sites), Performance % with green progress bar.

3.2 My Projects /pms/im-projects

Toolbar: Text search, Project Status filter, Team filter, Clear. Click a row or "View Details" to open the Project Detail page (five tabs: Overview, PO Lines, Rollout, Execution, Work Done).

3.3 My Teams /pms/im-teams

Table columns: Team ID, Team Name, Field User (team lead), Warehouse, Active POIDs, Status. A red dot badge on the My Teams nav link indicates pending Team Allocation Requests.

Team Detail panel (click a row)

TabContent
MembersRoster of all team members
StockCurrent stock in the team's warehouse broken down by DUID and item
Team Allocation RequestsIncoming requests from other IMs. Accept or Reject buttons with optional remark.

Edit team inline: Click the pencil icon on a row to edit Team Name and other details.

3.4 PO Control /pms/im-po-intake

The first IM step. Lines dispatched to you (auto or manually by the PM) land here before a target month is assigned.

Toolbar: Text search, Mode dropdown (All / Auto / Manual), All Projects, All DUIDs, Clear, Selection count + total SAR, Dispatch (N) (blue), Assign to Backend (N) (purple, backend-capable IMs only).

Table: Checkbox, POID, Mode badge (Auto=violet, Manual=slate), PO No, Project, Item, Description, Activity Type, Qty, Amount SAR, DUID, Center area, Dispatched On. Click anywhere on a row to toggle its checkbox.

Dispatch modal

  • Target month dropdown — rolling list (current month + next 11), required
  • After success: green toast "Moved N lines to My Dispatches (target month YYYY-MM)"

Assign to Backend modal (backend-capable IMs only)

  • Scrollable list of selected POIDs
  • Backend Team dropdown (required — lists active teams with category "Backend Team")
  • Note textarea (optional)
  • Error if selected lines include Closed or Completed POIDs

3.5 Rollout Planning /pms/im-dispatch

Create rollout plans for dispatched lines (lines with a target month assigned). Three tabs: New, In Progress, Completed.

The Create Plans modal is identical to the PM Planning modal (§2.7): Lead team, Visit type, Additional Teams with qty splitting, Access Details (start/end dates, access time, access period), QC Required / CIAG Required toggles, Remark textarea.

3.6 Rollout Execution /pms/im-planning

Monitor execution of your rollout plans in real time. Toolbar: Month filter, Status, Project, Team, DUID, Date range, Clear. Export Excel. Refresh.

Manager Remark inline edit: Click the remark cell to edit directly in the table. The remark is visible to field team on the execution form.

Plan Detail modal (View button)

  • Pills: Plan name, POID, Project, Team. Hero stats: Target, Achieved, Achievement %
  • Plan Teams Breakdown — all teams with assigned qty, TL status, achieved qty
  • Visit history — all visits for this POID
  • Attachments section — photos uploaded by field team
  • Remarks: general, manager (editable), team lead

3.7 Rollout Work Done /pms/im-execution

Review completed executions before finalisation. From the View button Detail Modal you can also Generate Work Done for completed plans.

3.8 Work Done /pms/im-work-done

Your finalised Work Done records with billing status from PIC. Toolbar: Billing Status filter (Pending / Invoiced / Closed), Team filter, Date range, Clear. Export Excel. Refresh. Billing status is updated automatically by PIC actions — no manual editing needed.

3.9 Issues & Risks /pms/im-issues-risks

Log and manage site-level issues for your teams. Create re-visit plans directly from issue rows — Visit Type pre-set to Re-Visit with an optional Issue Remarks textarea.

3.10 Material Management /pms/im-material-request

Manage the full materials chain for your teams. Four tabs:

Requests Tab

All active material transfer requests. Click a row to see POID, DUID, Team, Items table. Approve button — creates Material Transfer Stock Entry (main warehouse → team warehouse). Reject button — enter rejection remark.

DUID Stock Tab

Overview of DUIDs with materials available in main warehouse. Click Request on a DUID row to open the Create Transfer Request form:

SectionBehaviour
Huawei Materials (blue background)Auto-populates with items from this DUID's Huawei outbound receipt. Request Qty is editable (reduce from max). Cannot add new items.
Company Materials (green background)INET-owned materials. Search box to find items with current stock level shown (green if >0, red if 0). Add items with Qty and UOM.

Form fields: POID selector (auto-fills DUID and Team), Team dropdown, Remark (optional). A request can contain Huawei items only, Company items only, or both.

Returns Tab

Incoming return requests from your field teams. Click a row to see items and quantities. Approve Return button — creates Stock Entry (team warehouse → main warehouse). Reject with a remark.

All Requests Tab

Complete history of all material requests including completed and rejected, with full filter options.

3.11 Backend /pms/im-backend

⚠️
Visible only to IMs with backend assignment permission. The "Backend" nav link only appears if your IM Master record has "Can Assign Backend" enabled. Contact your Project Manager if you need this access.

PO dispatch lines assigned to backend (external partner) teams — direct completion flow without a Rollout Plan or field execution form.

Mark Work Done modal: List of selected POIDs + Completion Date (required) + Remark (optional). Confirm creates a synthesised Work Done record for each line.

3.12 Expense Approvals /pms/im-expense

Total SAR of filtered rows shown next to filter controls. Status badges: Pending · Approved · Rejected · Paid

Claim Detail panel (click a row)

  • Date, Team, Employee name, Total Claimed Amount
  • Expense Lines table: Expense Type, Description, Amount (SAR), POID(s) linked
  • Approve button — moves claim to "Unpaid" state on the field user's side
  • Reject button — enter a rejection reason (required; visible to the field user)
