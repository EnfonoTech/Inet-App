5.2 PIC Tracker /pms/pic-tracker

Your main daily working page. Every active PO line is here. Filter, edit, and bulk-update billing statuses.

Toolbar: Text search, PIC Status MS1 (multi-select), PIC Status MS2 (multi-select), All Projects, All DUIDs, Clear, Refresh, Download CSV, row limit selector.

Totals row at the bottom of the table sums all monetary columns over the current loaded rows.

Status pill colour coding

PurpleUnder I-BUY
Dark purpleUnder ISDP
AmberUnder Process to Apply / Pending
RedRejected / Cancelled
GreenInvoice Submitted / Closed / Accepted
ℹ️
Auto-status rule: Lines with no status manually set show "Under Process to Apply" when site work is done, or "Work Not Done" when it hasn't started. Once you set a status it is stored. To return to the automatic default, clear the field in the edit panel.

Edit a row — single-line editing

Click anywhere on a row to open the edit popover. Three tabs:

TabFields
MS1PIC Status (MS1) · I-BUY/ISDP Owner (free text) · Detail Remarks · MS1 Applied Date (validated: cannot be in the future) · MS1 iBuy Invoice Date (validated) · MS1 Invoice Month (month selector) · MS1 Payment Received Date (validated)
MS2Same fields as MS1 but for the MS2 milestone
AcceptanceSQC Status dropdown · PAT Status dropdown

Save / Cancel buttons. Validation errors shown inline.

Bulk Update

Select multiple rows → click Bulk Update (N). Modal: Milestone (MS1 or MS2 radio), Status dropdown, Remark (optional). Confirm updates all selected POIDs at once.

Download CSV

Exports all currently loaded rows as pic-tracker-YYYY-MM-DD.csv. Date fields trimmed to YYYY-MM-DD for spreadsheet compatibility.

5.3 Invoice Tracker /pms/pic-invoice-tracker

Focused view for lines at "Ready for Invoice" or beyond. Use this to create invoices and close milestones.

KPI pills (update with filters): MS1 Total · MS1 Invoiced · MS2 Total · MS2 Invoiced (SAR values across current view).

ActionWhat it does
Create Sales InvoiceDetects whether to invoice MS1 or MS2 for each selected line. Sets correct amounts and line references automatically. Creates Sales Invoice documents in ERPNext.
Mark SubmittedBulk-advance selected lines to "Commercial Invoice Submitted"
Mark ClosedBulk-advance selected lines to "Commercial Invoice Closed"
✅
Automatic status updates: When a Sales Invoice is submitted in ERPNext, the linked PO line automatically advances to "Commercial Invoice Submitted" and Work Done billing status becomes "Invoiced". No manual update required.

6. End-to-End Workflow Example

1
PM uploads PO file → Work order lines appear in the system.
↓
2
PM dispatches → Lines assigned to IMs with a target month (Auto or Manual mode).
↓
3
IM opens PO Control → Reviews incoming lines, assigns target month → lines move to Rollout Planning.
↓
4
IM creates rollout plan → Visit date, lead team, additional teams, QC/CIAG toggles, access time set. Field team sees the work in Today's Work on the plan date.
↓
5
IM receipts Huawei delivery → Creates Material Receipt Stock Entry from Huawei Outbound Plan.
↓
6
IM creates material transfer request → DUID Stock tab → fills Huawei and/or Company materials → submits → approves → stock moves to team warehouse.
↓
7
Field team executes on site → Opens Today's Work → taps plan card → starts timer → sets status to In Progress → fills achieved qty, materials used, QC/CIAG, GPS, photo, TL remarks → submits.
↓
8
Work Done created automatically when status is Completed → Revenue, cost, and margin calculated.
↓
9
PIC tracks billing → PIC Tracker → edits each line: MS1 Applied Date → I-BUY Owner → advances status: Under Process → Under I-BUY → Under ISDP → Ready for Invoice.
↓
10
PIC creates Sales Invoice → Invoice Tracker → select ready lines → Create Sales Invoice → one click generates the invoice.
↓
11
Invoice closed → Payment received → PIC marks "Commercial Invoice Closed". Work Done shows Closed. Dashboards reflect final numbers within 5 minutes.

7. Common Questions & Troubleshooting

Dashboard shows stale data
Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to force a full page reload. Dashboards auto-refresh every 5 minutes.
I can't see my teams or projects
Contact your system administrator to verify your user account, IM Master record, and team links.
PO Upload row shows an error
Verify the item code and project code exist in the Masters; contact your PM if unsure.
Work Done billing status stuck on Pending
The PIC team updates this as they process milestones — no action needed from your side.
Material Request button not showing for a DUID
All received items for that DUID have already been requested; remaining quantity is zero.
IM dashboard empty or showing wrong data
Contact your administrator to link your user account to your IM Master record and assign teams.
Backend nav link not showing in IM sidebar
Your IM Master record needs "Can Assign Backend" enabled. Contact your Project Manager.
Invoice Tracker "Create Sales Invoice" fails
Check that the Customer and Item Code are set on the PO line. Verify the item has a valid billing rate in Customer Item Master.
Return request submitted but stock hasn't moved
The IM needs to approve the return from the Returns tab in Material Requests.
Expense claim stuck on Pending
Your IM reviews claims from the Expense Approvals page. Follow up with them directly.
+ New Claim button is greyed out
Your user account must be linked to an Employee record. Contact your IM or administrator.
Expense claim not in IM's Approvals list
The claim routes to the IM assigned to your team. Verify your team has a correct IM assigned.
Team Allocation dot on IM sidebar
You have pending Team Allocation Requests — open My Teams to review and Accept or Reject.
Billing status shows Invoiced but I submitted a Draft invoice
Only Submitted (not Draft) Sales Invoices trigger the automatic status update. Submit the invoice in ERPNext first.
GPS fails to capture
Check that your browser has location permission. On mobile, ensure Location Services is enabled for the browser. The form allows manual coordinate entry as a fallback.
TL Remark templates not appearing
Templates are saved per-user. Type a remark in the picker and tap "+ Add" to create your first template.
Plan shows "QC Not Required" / "CIAG Not Required"
The PM or IM unchecked those workflow toggles when creating the plan. You do not need to fill QC/CIAG status for that plan.
"No materials assigned for this POID yet" in execution form
The IM has not yet approved a material transfer request for this work order. Contact your IM.
