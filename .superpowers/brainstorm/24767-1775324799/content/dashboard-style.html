IM PERFORMANCE

B — Excel-Faithful (Blue & White)

Closely mirrors the customer's existing Excel dashboard. Blue headers on white cells. Familiar grid layout. Lower learning curve — the team already knows this format. Clean, professional, corporate.

My Recommendation: Option A

  • Built for wall-mounted screens — dark theme reduces glare and eye strain
  • Color-coded values (green/yellow/red) pop against dark backgrounds
  • Looks like a proper operations center, not a spreadsheet
  • Professional upgrade from Excel that justifies the investment

Option B is also strong if...

  • The team is resistant to change and wants "just like Excel but online"
  • They print dashboards or view on low-quality monitors
  • Familiarity matters more than aesthetics
